Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from Destination Alert, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to this link to access the account deletion request form.
  2. Enter your account details and submit the form.
  3. You will receive a confirmation email once your request is processed.

What Data Will Be Deleted:

  • User profile information
  • Usage history
  • Personal preferences

For more information, please contact our support team at dalertapp@gmail.com.
